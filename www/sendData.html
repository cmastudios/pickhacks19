Patient Privacy

The privacy of your data is extremely important to FLEX. You and your healthcare provider can create a plan to choose what data you need to share. In addition, if you would like your FLEX data to contribute to research in physical therapy, you can choose to submit your information to a database that is accessible by physical therapists conducting research across the world to discover how best to heal from physical injuries.
